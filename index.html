How it works
STEP 01

Enable hotspot

Turn on Mobile Hotspot on your Android device and connect your PC, tablet, or other phone to it.

STEP 02

Tap START

The app creates a local SOCKS5 proxy tunnel and shows you the IP address and port to use.

STEP 03

Configure & connect

Scan the QR code or enter the IP and port in your SOCKS5 client on any connected device.

STEP 04

Browse freely

Traffic routes through your GSM connection. No carrier quota detection, no remote server involved.

Features

One-tap tunnel

START launches the proxy immediately. No configuration wizards, no accounts, no cloud dependency.

QR code setup

Tap "Show QR" and point your phone's camera. Host and port fill in automatically on compatible clients.

Fully local

No remote VPN server. No traffic logging. Every packet stays within your own hardware chain.

IP change detection

Carrier IPs shift between sessions. The app detects changes and alerts connected devices automatically.
